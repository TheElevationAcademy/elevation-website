Fusion Course: Hindustani Classical Music X Bollywood Singing

12 Months | 44 Live Sessions | Personalized Learning

Course Overview

This unique fusion course blends the rich tradition of Hindustani Classical Music with the contemporary appeal of Bollywood singing. Over 12 months, you'll receive 44 live one-on-one sessions with an expert mentor, covering both technical foundations and practical application in popular music.

Course Structure

  • Duration: 12 months (1 year)
  • Sessions: 44 live one-on-one classes
  • Frequency: 1 live class per week with your mentor
  • Format: Individual classes only (personalized attention)

What You'll Learn

Practical Singing

  • Learn any 7 songs of your choice
  • Bollywood singing techniques
  • Pitch matching and notations

Technical Foundations

  • Saptak and Alankar practice
  • Swar - Shuddha, Komal and Teevra
  • Taal and Laya concepts

Raag Studies

  • Raag Yaman core concepts
  • Raag Bhimpalasi core concepts
  • Application of raags in Bollywood music

Detailed Curriculum

  1. Song Selection: Master 7 songs of your choice with proper technique
  2. Vocal Exercises: Saptak (octave) practice and Alankars (ornamentations)
  3. Pitch Training: Develop perfect pitch through matching exercises and notation reading
  4. Swar Knowledge: Mastery of Shuddha (natural), Komal (flat), and Teevra (sharp) notes
  5. Raag Yaman: In-depth study of this evening raag, its structure and characteristics
  6. Raag Bhimpalasi: Comprehensive understanding of this afternoon raag
  7. Rhythm Concepts: Taal (cycle) and Laya (tempo) theory and practice

Why Choose This Course?

This unique fusion approach gives you the best of both worlds - the discipline and depth of classical music combined with the versatility and appeal of Bollywood singing. The one-on-one format ensures personalized attention and progress at your own pace.
