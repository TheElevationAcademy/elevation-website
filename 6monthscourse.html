Meet the Co-Founders

🎙️ Karishma Tamane

Co-Founder, Vocal Coach & Creative Strategist

Karishma is a passionate vocalist and creative entrepreneur with years of experience in Hindustani classical and contemporary singing. A performer and educator at heart, she blends musical tradition with modern teaching to empower students globally.

She’s mentored hundreds of learners, developed transformative curriculums, and actively contributes to audio production and original music. At Elevation Academy, she leads student interaction and creative direction.

🎧 Koushik Mukherjee

Co-Founder, Music Producer & Technical Head

Koushik brings technical brilliance and musical artistry to the Elevation Academy. As a music producer and multi-instrumentalist, he crafts rich audio experiences and guides students through professional-grade recording and production.

With deep knowledge in DAWs, mixing, and sound design, he ensures every song, session, and release meets world-class quality while making students' ideas come alive.

🎼 Together at The Elevation Academy

Karishma and Koushik co-founded The Elevation Academy with a vision to make music learning deeply personal, joyful, and effective. Whether you’re a beginner or a budding artist, their combined strengths will elevate your musical journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the class timings? How much time is required per week? ⌄
We offer flexible batch timings. You’ll have 1 live session per week and daily guided practice material.
I’m a complete beginner. Will I be able to learn online? ⌄
Absolutely! Our step-by-step teaching approach is perfect for beginners.
What style of music do you teach? ⌄
Hindustani Classical, Bollywood, Semi-Classical, and Ghazals with a contemporary touch.
Do I get practice material or recordings after class? ⌄
Yes! Each session comes with a recording and customized riyaaz assignments.
Will I get a certificate after the course? ⌄
Yes, we provide a digital certificate on successful course completion.
What is included in the free trial session? ⌄
The trial includes a one-on-one vocal assessment, introduction to our curriculum, and live guidance from our team.
Are the sessions live or recorded? ⌄
All sessions are live and interactive. Recordings are provided afterward for practice.
Can I get personal attention or feedback? ⌄
Yes, we provide personalized feedback in every session to help you improve faster.
What if I miss a session? ⌄
You’ll get access to the recording and support material so you can catch up easily.
Can I get a professional recording of my singing? ⌄
Yes! We offer free recordings as part of our 3/6/12-month programs and guide you through the process.
