About the Academy

Elevation Academy is an online vocal training space built exclusively for adult learners who are passionate about singing but may have never had the chance to explore their voice fully. Whether you're a beginner or someone who sings casually, we help you grow with the right blend of personalized attention, Indian classical fundamentals, and modern music techniques.

We don't just teach you what to sing – we help you discover how to sing, with confidence and clarity.
