Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

The Elevation Academy ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our vocal training services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, WhatsApp details
  • Payment Details: UPI IDs, transaction history (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Vocal Data: Audio recordings, progress notes, feedback sessions
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, class attendance logs
  • Preferences: Musical interests, availability, learning goals

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized vocal training services
  • Process payments and send course materials
  • Improve curriculum and teaching methods
  • Communicate about schedule changes or new offerings
  • Ensure quality control through session recordings
  • Comply with legal obligations

Audio Recordings Policy

All training sessions are recorded for:

  • Student self-review
  • Progress tracking
  • Quality assurance

Recordings are stored securely for 6 months post-course completion.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay/UPI providers (only necessary transaction data)
  • Technical Providers: Google Workspace for emails, Zoom for classes
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Indian law

4. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encrypted WhatsApp communications
  • Password-protected session recordings
  • Secure payment gateways (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Regular system audits

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of non-essential data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Requests can be made to: thelevationacademy@gmail.com

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance.
